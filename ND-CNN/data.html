Newcastle Disease

Clinical signs of Newcastle Disease can vary depending on the strain and species of the affected birds. Common symptoms include
respiratory distress (coughing, sneezing, nasal discharge), nervous signs (tremors, paralysis), digestive disturbances (diarrhea, greenish feces), decreased egg production, and high mortality rates.
